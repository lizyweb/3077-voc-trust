Who We Are

Established in 2013 under the Indian Trust Act of 1882, VOC Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering sustainable development and improving the quality of life for communities across India. Our initiatives span various sectors, including agriculture, herbal farming, millet promotion, women's empowerment, elder care, sanitation, and environmental conservation.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To implement innovative and impactful programs that enhance livelihoods, promote gender equality, and ensure environmental sustainability for the betterment of the community.

Our Vision

To create a self-reliant and empowered society where every individual has access to basic necessities and opportunities for sustainable development.

Our Objectives

Disaster Management

Provide immediate relief and support to affected communities.
Develop and implement disaster preparedness programs.
Establish early warning systems to mitigate the impact of disasters.

Women Empowerment

Promote gender equality and women's rights.
Provide education and vocational training for women.
Support women's economic independence through entrepreneurship programs.
Advocate for women's health and reproductive rights.
Create safe spaces and support networks for women facing violence or discrimination.

Juvenile Welfare

Ensure the protection and rights of children and adolescents.
Provide access to quality education and extracurricular activities.
Support the mental and physical health of juveniles.
Implement rehabilitation programs for at-risk youth.
Advocate for child-friendly policies and legal frameworks.

Agro-Based Socio-Economic Development

Enhance the livelihood of rural farmers through sustainable agricultural practices.
Provide training and resources for modern farming techniques.
Promote agro-based industries and value addition to agricultural products.

Garbage Waste Management

Promote waste segregation at the source for better recycling.
Implement efficient and eco-friendly waste collection and disposal systems.
Educate the community on the importance of reducing, reusing, and recycling waste.

Save Water - Rain Water Harvesting

Promote the adoption of rainwater harvesting systems in urban and rural areas.
Educate communities on the importance of water conservation.
Provide technical assistance and resources for building rainwater harvesting structures.
Collaborate with local governments to implement water conservation policies.
Monitor and evaluate the impact of water harvesting projects on water availability.

Public Toilets - Sanitation Progress

Increase access to clean and safe public toilets in urban and rural areas.
Promote hygiene education and awareness campaigns.
Collaborate with local authorities to build and maintain sanitation facilities.
Implement programs to ensure the regular cleaning and maintenance of public toilets.

Tree Sapling and Regular Maintenance

Promote a forestation and reforestation efforts in deforested areas.
Educate communities on the importance of tree planting for environmental sustainability.
Provide saplings and technical support for tree planting initiatives.

Solar Energy for Affordable Housing Projects

Promote the use of solar energy in affordable housing projects.
Provide technical and financial assistance for installing solar panels.
Educate communities on the benefits of renewable energy.
Collaborate with government and private sector partners to scale up solar energy adoption.

Free Food Distribution and Integrated Asylum

Ensure food security for vulnerable and marginalized populations.
Provide regular free meals to those in need.
Integrate food distribution programs with shelters and asylum services.
Promote community kitchens and food banks.
Collaborate with local businesses and farmers to source surplus food.

Drug Addiction and De-Addiction Centre

Provide comprehensive treatment and rehabilitation services for individuals with drug addiction.
Educate communities on the dangers of drug abuse and prevention strategies.
Offer counselling and support services for recovering addicts and their families.
Collaborate with healthcare providers to ensure holistic care.
Advocate for policies and programs that support addiction recovery and reintegration into society.

Animal Welfare

Promote the humane treatment of animals.
Provide rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption services for stray and abandoned animals.
Advocate for stronger animal protection laws and enforcement.
Educate the community on responsible pet ownership and animal rights.

Our Programs

Sustainable Agriculture

Our agricultural development programs focus on introducing and promoting sustainable farming techniques. We work with local farmers to cultivate herbals and millets, ensuring they have access to modern practices and resources that increase yield and income.

Herbals and Millets Promotion

Recognizing the health benefits and economic potential of herbals and millets, we conduct extensive training sessions and workshops for farmers. These programs aim to enhance knowledge and skills in cultivating these crops, which are not only nutritious but also resilient to climate change.

Women Empowerment

We are committed to empowering women by providing them with education, vocational training, and support for small business ventures. Our programs help women gain financial independence and leadership roles within their communities.

Elders Home

Our elder care program offers a safe and supportive environment for senior citizens. We provide comprehensive care, including health services, recreational activities, and emotional support to ensure a dignified and fulfilling life for the elderly.

Free Toilets for National Highways

In our effort to improve public health and sanitation, we construct free toilets along national highways. This initiative aims to reduce open defecation, promote hygiene, and provide travelers with necessary facilities.

Rainwater Harvesting

To address the critical issue of water scarcity, we promote rainwater harvesting techniques. Our programs involve community education and the installation of rainwater harvesting systems to conserve and efficiently use water resources.
